Claudesop

Personal content scheduling tool for TikTok.

Terms of Service

This tool is for personal use only. It allows the authenticated owner to schedule and upload content to their own TikTok account. No third-party users are involved. By using this tool, you agree to comply with TikTok's Terms of Service.

Privacy Policy

This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data from TikTok users. It only accesses the authenticated account owner's profile to upload pre-produced content via the TikTok Content Posting API. No analytics, tracking, or data storage is performed on any user other than the authenticated owner.
